The Analysis and Design...

The following are the key points behind the design of this web system...

Business Purpose

Our main goal is try to ease your burden of having a pet. Sometimes unexpected events can interrupt your day and make it hard to take care of your pets. Literally under any situation when you find it hard to focus on both your life and your pets, our service is here for you. Diverse activities and facilities available at our hotel can provide exclusive experience for your pets. Medical support and insurance is also in place, guarding the safety of your pets is always our first priority.




Designs' Focus and User Needs

We noted the main user needs and expectations for the Web system for pet boarding services as a result of the analysis from the survey. They are easy booking facilities, supporting diverse ranges of pets, good feedback trapping system, active contact support, etc. We achieved an easy and efficient navigation system, proper hierarchy for information layout, mindful use of tooltips to achieve the strong law of usability and follow the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) theory for our web design.




Futrue Prospect

We are currently working on expanding the diversity of pets supported, number of hotel sites and different service options. Don't be frustrated if you find your pet is not supported or there is no nearby hotel at your location at the moment. Write down your need and suggestions to us and your opinions are the most valuable assets for us!
