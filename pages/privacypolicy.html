Privacy Policy

Data usage

To provide you with high-quality service and ease your use of the websites, some personal identifying information will be collected including but not limiting your name, living address and card details. By filling the blank of our booking process and clicking next button, you are confirming your consent to the data collected. No third party nor our analytic department will proceed your data into additional analysis in terms of advertisement or recommendation without asking for your consent. In the meantime, cookies will be used to identify the user. However, it is totally up to you whether to accept it or not.




Your Exclusive Rights

We value your every right on your information and every personal details will only be collected with your consent. You can choose whether to use cookies and personal recommendations in regard of service or discounts you may be interested. However, some essential details such as banking information or details about your pets are necessary for our service and you can choose to withdraw anytime during the booking process. Your banking details will not be stored anywhere and account information will be stored in our security database away from any potential damage or leakage.
