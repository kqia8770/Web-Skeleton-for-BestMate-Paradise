Housing for Other Pets...

Housing for Little Rabbits

We currently possess four dorm rooms for rabbits and each of them can contain up to 5 rabbits.

Separated living environment is not guaranteed, but we are working to make that come true.

Future expansion of rooms can be expected.

Housing for Little Ponies

Our stable can now contain 6 horses therefore rooms may be occupied during hot seasons.

We also have an open grass field for horses to run in.

You can choose to ride your pony and have a friendly race against others in our special activities.


Housing for Little Birdies

We now have one large yard with an over cage available for birds.

Currently, we do not have many activities available since it is a newly opened category.

Do let us know about your ideas via feedback!

We are currently working on expanding the ranges of pets we can support.

Further construction and improvement is in process.

Do not hesitate to tell us what you think via feedback blocks!

Your support decides our future!
